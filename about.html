KASIT College

King Abdullah II College of Information Technology was established in 2000 under the directives of His Majesty King Abdullah II Bin Al Hussein to develop the information technology sector in Jordan and the region. Based on the objectives and aspirations of the King Abdullah II College of Information Technology, the college has worked to prepare scientific cadres specialized in the field of information technology to provide the local, Arab and international market, as well as the college to provide academic programs and laboratories equipped to achieve its vision, which keeps pace with the latest developments in the world of information technology. * King Abdullah II College of Information Technology includes the following academic departments: 1- Computer Science Department 2- Computer Information Systems Department 3- Information Technology Department * The College offers the following degrees: 1- Bachelor Degree in Computer Science 2- Bachelor Degree in Computer Information Systems 3 - Bachelor Degree in Information Technology 4 - Master Degree in Computer Science 5 - Master Degree in Information Systems 6 - Master Degree in Network Intelligence 7 - Master Degree in E - Government 8 - Doctorate Degree in Computer Science * Major Achievements In the center (301-350) annex The world's best universities in the Specialization "Computer Science and Computer Information Systems" by rating the global QS. - Obtained the National Accreditation for all its academic programs and obtained the global accreditation from the American Council of Engineering and Technology ABET, for the programs of Bachelor of Computer Science and Bachelor of Computer Information Systems, as it completed the requirements for obtaining the global accreditation ABET program for the Bachelor of Business Information Technology to be the first college In Jordan, all undergraduate programs are accredited globally by ABET. - The college contains (23) laboratories specialized in information technology and (17) halls to serve the academic requirements.
